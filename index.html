About Us

PT. Mega Energi Digdaya Indonesia

is an Oil n Gas and Fire Fighting Equipments and Fire Fighting Delevery Hoses and services in Construction, Project Management, Engineering, Procerument/Logistic, Transportation, Catering, Manpower supply company in Indonesia.

We are in the upstream and downstream of O&G business providing crews, supplies and services to mainly offshore and onshore. We are in progress to have a certificate of ISO 9001 : 2015 and OHSAS 18001:2007.

PT Mega Energi Digdaya Indonesia (PT. MEDI) is also one of the companies within Dwiguna Group, Registered in Depok, West Java, Indonesia. Dwiguna Group itself was established in May of 2007, which consists in the end of several companies.

We are dedicated to providing EPC, Transportation, Catering, Manpower, Procurement, Supply and Services for Energy Industries including Oil & Gas (Onshore & Offshore Drilling, Geothermal and Marine); to become a new leading in the Oil and Gas Industry, Fire Fighting Equipments and Fire Fighting Delevery Hoses

PT. MEDI conducts its business focused on our core values, PRECISE (Professionalism, Responsible, Expert, Cooperative, Innovative, Smart and Excellent).
